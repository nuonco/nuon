There are many common issues and challenges you will run into architecting, deploying, and maintaining a BYOC app. This guide will walk you through best practices for addressing these, to set you up for success with your customers.

## Introduction

This guide is broken up into 4 parts.

1. Configuration: Ensuring your app is configured per best practices for each stage of the app lifecycle.
1. Customer Setup: Ensuring you are prepared to deploy your app to the customer's environment.
1. Considerations: Key things to consider when building and maintaining a BYOC app.
1. FAQ: Common questions you may have.

This next few pages will walk you through configuring your app to meet best practices at each stage of packaing, deploying, and maintaining a BYOC app.
For each stage, we will highlight key considerations, and provide a checklist you can follow to make sure all your bases are covered.

After that, we will dive into details around customer environments, updating your app, and other considerations.
