<Note>
This guide assumes you have onboarded to the Nuon platform, and are familiar with it's basic concepts.
If you haven't done that yet, we recommend completing the [Quickstart](/get-started/quickstart) first.
</Note>

There are many common issues and challenges you will run into architecting, deploying, and maintaining an [App](/concepts/apps). This guide will walk you through best practices for addressing these, to set you up for success with your customers.

## Introduction

This guide is broken up into 4 parts.

<Steps>
    <Step title="Configuration">Ensuring your [App](/concepts/apps) is configured per best practices for each stage of the App lifecycle.</Step>
    <Step title="Customer Setup">Ensuring you are prepared to deploy your App to the customer's environment.</Step>
    <Step title="Considerations">Key things to consider when building and maintaining a App.</Step>
    <Step title="FAQ">Common questions you may have.</Step>
</Steps>

The next few pages will walk you through configuring your App to meet best practices at each stage of packaging, deploying, and maintaining an App.
For each stage, we will highlight important details, and provide a checklist you can follow to make sure all your bases are covered.

After that, we will dive into details around customer environments, updating your App, and other considerations.
