There are many common issues and challenges you will run into architecting, deploying, and maintaining a BYOC app. This guide will walk you through best practices for addressing these, to set you up for success with your customers.

## Introduction

This guide is broken up into 4 parts.

<Steps>
    <Step title="Configuration">Ensuring your app is configured per best practices for each stage of the app lifecycle.</Step>
    <Step title="Customer Setup">Ensuring you are prepared to deploy your app to the customer's environment.</Step>
    <Step title="Considerations">Key things to consider when building and maintaining a BYOC app.</Step>
    <Step title="FAQ">Common questions you may have.</Step>
</Steps>

The next few pages will walk you through configuring your app to meet best practices at each stage of packaging, deploying, and maintaining a BYOC app.
For each stage, we will highlight important details, and provide a checklist you can follow to make sure all your bases are covered.

After that, we will dive into details around customer environments, updating your app, and other considerations.
